✶ Process of Commissioning Me

Reserve

  • to reserve your slot, you must accomplish the commission form:
    • local clients
    • international clients
  • upon completion, kindly contact me thru any of my social media accs to send a screenshot of the accomplished form :>

Secure

  • to secure your slot, you may pay 50% or 100% of the agreed amount upfront via:
    • GCash; and
    • PayPal;
  • within 48 hours upon reserving of your slot, otherwise will lead to forfeiting the slot.
  • Note: Invoice will be provided (docx. file / PayPal Invoice)

Draft Review

  • in this stage, i will be providing the client a rough sketch/draft of the artwork.
  • the client is entitled to:
    • 2 revisions within the agreed scope
    • timely feedback to maintain project timeline.
  • Note: additional revisions will be subject to extra fees

Coloring and Rendering

  • in this stage, the client is entitled to:
    • 1 revision of flat colors
  • Any revision when the rendering process has started will not be entertained.

Completion

  • In this stage, the client must settle their remaining balance (if applicable), otherwise will be denied access to the final artwork.
  • upon completion, the artist will send the final artwork through Google Drive containing:
    • PNG and JPEG file of the artwork
    • Invoice of payment
  • please be reminded that:
    • the client must use the artwork only as agreed—either for personal (non-commercial) use or commercial use.
    • any use of the artwork beyond the agreed scope without prior written permission from the artist is prohibited and considered a breach of these terms.
    • The artist reserves the right to take appropriate actions if the terms of use are violated.

Artist Comfort & Right to Decline Commission

  • The artist reserves the right to decline or cancel any commission request at their discretion.
  • If a commission request does not clearly fall within the Do's, or if it falls under the Don'ts, it will be respectfully declined.

Copyright & Usage Rights

  • The commissioned work by the client is for personal use only. Any usage intended beyond the agreed scope, additional fees apply (commercial use/license).
  • The artist retains full copyright of the illustration, while the client owns the artwork.
  • The client agrees to credit the artist when displaying the commissioned artwork publicly, whenever possible.
  • The client agrees not to resell, redistribute, or claim the artwork as their own.

Quote & Payment

  • Payments for commissions are accepted only via GCash or PayPal. Please confirm with me which method you will use before sending any payment. Kindly message me for the account details.
  • A 50% non-refundable deposit is required before I begin the actual drawing of the commission. You can also do 100% upfront (optional).
  • If the client cancels the commission after work has begun, the artist reserves the right to retain payment proportionate to work completed.
  • If the artist needs to cancel, the client will be refunded any payments made.
